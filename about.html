Bhakalo Where shopping is a pleasure !

Bhakalo is a retail shop of fruits, vegetables, dry fruits, juices, ice-cream, milk products, cookies, chocolates and all groceries.

Bhakalo was started in November 2005 in Solapur, Maharashtra, India. This type of concept of all fruits, vegetables, dry fruits, bakery stuff, juices and all groceries under one roof is first in Maharashtra. We covered 150km. radius of area near Solapur. We are giving best quality of all groceries and garden fresh fruits and vegetables.
